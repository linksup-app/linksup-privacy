Privacy Policy

Last updated: April 6, 2026

Links Up Golf ("we," "our," or "the app") is a mobile application that helps golfers coordinate tee times and connect with their golf groups. Your privacy matters to us, and this policy explains what information we collect, how we use it, and your rights.

Information We Collect

When you create an account and use Links Up, we collect the following information:

  • Account information: Your email address, first and last name, and password (stored securely by Firebase Authentication).
  • Profile information: Your golf handicap and profile photo, which you provide voluntarily.
  • Location data: Your home city, state, and coordinates (entered manually or detected via your device's GPS with your permission). We use this to display local weather forecasts and suggest nearby golf courses.
  • Group and activity data: Golf groups you create or join, tee times, availability blocks, and related coordination data.
  • Device tokens: Push notification tokens to deliver tee time reminders and group notifications.

How We Use Your Information

  • To provide and operate the app's core features: group coordination, tee time scheduling, availability sharing, and weather forecasts.
  • To send you push notifications about tee times, group invites, and reminders you've opted into.
  • To display your profile to other members of your golf groups.
  • To improve the app experience and fix issues.

How We Store Your Data

Your data is stored using Google Firebase services (Firestore, Firebase Authentication, and Firebase Cloud Storage), hosted on Google Cloud infrastructure. All data is transmitted over encrypted HTTPS connections. We do not sell, rent, or share your personal information with third parties for marketing purposes.

Third-Party Services

Links Up uses the following third-party services:

  • Firebase (Google): Authentication, database, file storage, and push notifications. Firebase Privacy Policy
  • OpenWeatherMap: Weather forecast data based on your location coordinates. No personal information is shared with OpenWeatherMap beyond geographic coordinates. OpenWeatherMap Privacy Policy

Location Data

Links Up requests access to your device's location to provide weather forecasts and suggest nearby courses. Location permission is optional. If you decline, you can manually search for your city instead. We do not track your location in the background or store location history.

Data Retention

We retain your data for as long as your account is active. When you delete your account (available in Settings within the app), we permanently delete your profile, group memberships, tee times, availability data, and profile photo from our systems.

Your Rights

  • Access and update: You can view and edit your profile information, handicap, and home location directly in the app.
  • Delete your account: You can permanently delete your account and all associated data from Settings in the app.
  • Manage notifications: You can enable or disable push notifications through your device's Settings.
  • Location control: You can revoke location access at any time in your device's Settings.

Children's Privacy

Links Up is not directed at children under the age of 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children. If we become aware that we have collected data from a child under 13, we will delete it promptly.

Changes to This Policy

We may update this privacy policy from time to time. If we make significant changes, we will notify you through the app or by other means. Your continued use of the app after changes are posted constitutes your acceptance of the updated policy.
