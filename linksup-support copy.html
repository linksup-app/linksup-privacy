Need help with Links Up? We're here for you.

Common Questions

How do I join a group? Ask the group owner for their join code, then enter it on the Groups tab. You can also join during onboarding.

How do I invite friends? Open your group, tap "Share Invite Link" to send the join code via iMessage or any messaging app.

How do I delete my account? Go to Profile, tap Settings, and scroll to "Delete Account." This permanently removes all your data.

Weather isn't showing for my area? Make sure your home location is set in your Profile, or tap the location label on the home screen to set a temporary location.
